12. Saskatchewan Renovation Tax Credit —  Clarification 
Management clarified that the Saskatchewan renovation tax credit applies to individual unit owners for their 
proportionate share of eligible condo renovation expenses. 
Management advised a receipt/memo will be issued for owners to provide to their tax accountants.
